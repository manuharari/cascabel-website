01 — The Challenge

200 units.
One Excel that never balanced.

Every month, the team manually compared bank statements against receipts from PDFs, WhatsApp, and email. There was no single source of truth, no clear references from tenants, and no consistent matching rules.

The books wouldn't close until the 20th or later, often with errors that triggered complaint calls. Two major owners were already considering switching firms.

"On the 15th, we were still hunting down payments. Owners would call, and I had no way to give them an answer."

— Director of Operations
02 — The Solution

Concilia:
Rules, not intuition.

An app designed for reality: in Mexico, automatic bank integrations are rare. The team downloads their bank statement as usual and uploads it to Concilia. From there, the system does what used to take days.

Define reconciliation rules once—which tenant pays which amount, on what dates, with which references—and apply them automatically every month.

For unmatched payments, Concilia doesn't just flag them: it suggests what to do—whether to assign it to the closest tenant by amount, issue an underpayment notice, or move it to a pending account. The team decides; the tool eliminates the guesswork.

Upon closing, it generates each owner’s financial statement in PDF, ready to send.

Implementation took five months: diagnosis, building, a pilot run alongside the old process, and two live closings until stabilized.

03 — The Impact

From the 20th
to day 6.

Monthly closing dropped from 12–18 business days to 4–6. Financial statements now reach owners in the first week of the month. Errors fell from 10–15 per cycle to just 1–2.

The two owners who had considered leaving renewed their contracts for an additional two years.

"I used to dread the end of the month. Now the team closes it in four days, and the owners no longer call to complain."

— Director of Operations
